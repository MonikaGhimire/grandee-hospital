About Us

Grande International Hospital, Kathmandu is a multi-specialty hospital that offers preventive and curative healthcare services. It was established with a motive 'Care to Cure' in February 2010. It began as an idea of a patient-oriented, socially responsible center, making superior quality healthcare services convenient and accessible for the community it serves.
